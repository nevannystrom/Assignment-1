# Assignment-1

## Exercise Descriptions

### Exercise 1 – Profit Margin Calculator
The program asks the user for revenue and cost, calculates profit and profit margin, and prints the results as dollars and a percentage.

### Exercise 2 – Credit Score Evaluator
The program asks for a credit score and determines the loan eligibility category and message.

### Exercise 3 – Customer Greeting Formatter
Formats a greeting by cleaning the name, getting the first name, and adding a title.

### Exercise 4 – Tax Bracket Determiner
Determines a tax bracket based on income and calculates estimated tax.

### Exercise 5 – Product Category Matcher
The program asks for a product name, cleans the text, and assigns a category based on the type of product.

### Bonus – Integrated Decision Tool
Checks if a business is profitable and gives a suggestion based on the product category.
